About Us

We are Team of Professional Photographers and Videographers from Aurangabad, Maharashtra. We film Weddings, Commercials & what not.

ANUJ JANGLE
(FOUNDER & DIRECTOR)

A man with many passions. Passions for Photography, Cinematography, Music & Art. Photography was my main such passion which eventually turned into my career. It was something I could do every day and could never get tired of. Apart from this, I like to connect with emotions and stories which apparently, brought me into covering the greatest emotions of all time.

In these 3 years of my life, I’ve made lots of videos and Collaborated with great brands around the Globe
