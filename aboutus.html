About Us

ANUJ JANGLE
(FOUNDER & CEO)


A man with many passions. Passions for Photography, Cinematography, Music & Art. Photography was my main such passion which eventually turned into my career. It was something I could do every day and could never get tired of. Apart from this, I like to connect with emotions and stories which apparently, brought me into covering the greatest emotions of all time. These are the moments I feel must be preserved in the treasure box of memories of the people living them. Taking my passion forward, I took little steps which brought FILMit Productions into existence. I did start it alone but now what I have is a beautiful team who strives to offer our clients the best of their memories.

In these 3 years of my life, I’ve made lots of videos and Collaborated with great brands around the Globe
